Dear MS/HS Parents, We are excited to invite you to campus for our first-ever HS music concert on Friday, June 8th at 17h00 in the PAC. We will have the pleasure of enjoying a performance on Friday evening from both our HS Band and HS Chorus who will entertain you with music from several different genres. For your planning, please note that the show is expected to last roughly 90 minutes. Please note that even if your child is not a member of the HS Band or Chorus, you are still welcome to enjoy the concert and the wonderful music by our talented students. The event is, of course, optional. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact the secondary school office (secondary@sjajeju.kr, 064-801-1306). We hope to see many of you in attendance on Friday evening in two weeks' time.

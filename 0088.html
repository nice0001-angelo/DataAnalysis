Dear MS/HS Parents, Our uniform vendor, Ivy Club, has apologetically identified an issue with the length of our skirts, and realizes that they are not in compliance with our dress code. As such, they've offered to do free alternations over the summer for all female skirts in order for them to be of appropriate length for school. This is a requirement for virtually all female skirts sold this year of which most are too short for a school environment. Ivy Club will contact you with specific details directly in order to let you know how to take advantage of this service. Please note that beginning next school year, we will strictly enforce dress code as outlined in our previous communication with parents. Any female students arriving at school in skirts that are not knee-length, will not be allowed to attend classes until they are in compliance with dress code. You are asked to take the first step in ensuring that your child is appropriately dressed for school next year. Female students are can also wear uniform pants, instead of a skirt, as long as the material is the same gray color as our current uniform. Thank you in advance for your help and for taking care of this over the summer in preparation for next school year.
